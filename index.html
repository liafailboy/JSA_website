日本の文化

Our goal is to spread Japanese Culture in Georgia Tech Community and around Atlanta. We are welcoming people from all backgrounds.

イベント

We are planning to hold a lot of interesting events throughout semesters. Events include cook-out, going to restaurants, welcoming events and etc.

コミュニティ

Our community is spread around Georgia Tech, including Japan Club from the colleges close to Georgia Tech. We also aim to join in Japan Fest from next time.

参加方法

If you want to participate in one of the event we are holding, you can either register with Facebook to JSA or email us from the contact.
